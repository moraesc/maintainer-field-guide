# The AI Contributor

The AI Contributor is a new type of contributor that uses artificial intelligence to generate code contributions. These contributors leverage large language models and other AI tools to create pull requests, fix bugs, and add features to open source projects.

AI Contributors can be identified by their tendency to produce code that looks correct at first glance but may contain subtle issues. Their commit messages are often well-formatted and professional, sometimes suspiciously so.

The impact of AI Contributors on the open source ecosystem is still being studied, but early observations suggest they can both help and hinder project development depending on the quality of their contributions and the oversight provided by human maintainers.

## Characteristics

- Uses AI tools to generate code
- May not fully understand the codebase
- Can produce large volumes of contributions quickly
- Contributions vary widely in quality
